Araku Valley

Araku Valley is a picturesque hill station located in the Eastern Ghats of India, lying 111 km west of Visakhapatnam city. It is a valley in the Eastern Ghats inhabited by different tribes, mainly Araku Tribes. The valley is surrounded by hills and has lush green landscapes that captivate visitors. Araku is renowned for its coffee plantations, producing some of the finest coffee beans.

Tirupati is a small city located in the southern state of Andhra Pradesh in India. It is known as a pilgrimage center with many famous temples dedicated to the incarnations of Lord Vishnu. The most popular temple in Tirupati is the Sri Venkateswara Temple, which is also known as the Temple of Seven Hills. It is located on the Tirupati peak and is considered to be the abode of Vishnu on Earth.

Srisaila Mallikarjuna temple

Srisailam is a town located in the Nandyal district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is a place of great spiritual significance and natural beauty. The town is home to the Sri Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple, which is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva and one of the eighteen Shakti Peethas of Goddess Parvati.Other popular tourist attractions in Srisailam include the Srisailam Dam, which is one of South India’s largest dams, and the Srisailam Tiger Reserve, which is India’s largest tiger reserve.

  • Sri Kalyana Venkateswaraswami Temple
  • Sri Govindarajaswami Temple
  • Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple
  • Sri Kapileswaraswami Temple
  • Srisailam Dam

Godavari

Rajahmundry is a city located in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is the fifth most populated city in the state. The city is known for its floriculture, history, culture, agriculture, economy, tourism, and its heritage. The Godavari River, which is the second-longest river in India, is a popular tourist attraction in Rajahmundry. Papi Kondalu, a scenic gorge and hills located near Rajahmundry, is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The Dowleswaram Barrage, an irrigation water control structure located near Rajahmundry, is a popular tourist attraction and offers a beautiful view of the Godavari River.

  • Godavari River
  • Papi Kondalu
  • Dowleswaram Barrage
  • ISKCON Temple
  • River bay
